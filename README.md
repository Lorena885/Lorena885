## 🌸Hey there, Welcome to my github!

I’m passionate about turning data into meaningful insights and helping teams make better decisions. With a background in Business Management and a Diploma in Data Science, I enjoy combining analytical thinking with practical business understanding.

Here, you'll find:

- 📊 Data analysis projects using Python and SQL 
- 📈 Dashboards and visualizations built with Power BI
- 🤖 Machine learning models for prediction and customer behavior analysis
- 📚 Learning notes and projects from my Data Science journey
- 🌱 Bits of inspiration for anyone trying to survive—and thrive—in the data jungle  

Most of my work focuses on real business datasets — exploring KPIs, understanding customer behavior, predicting churn, and improving decision-making processes.
